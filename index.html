Selective Verification of Erasure from LLM Answers (SVELA)

2026 - Bari, Italy

EVALITA 2026 Evaluation Campaign

Welcome to SVELA, a task dedicated to the Selective Verification of Erasure from LLM Answers, part of the EVALITA 2026 Evaluation Campaign in Bari, Italy.

This task focuses on evaluating machine unlearning in Large Language Models (LLMs) by assessing whether models have successfully "forgotten" specific information. The challenge addresses the critical need for reliable evaluation methods in machine unlearning, particularly in the context of privacy regulations like GDPR's "right to be forgotten", bias mitigation, and copyright protection. The task introduces innovative instance-level evaluation across multiple languages, challenging participants to develop metrics that can distinguish between retained, forgotten, and unseen data based solely on model behavior.
